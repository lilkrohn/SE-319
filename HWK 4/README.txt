To compile and run my program, follow the instructions below:

For task 1: 
- Import and then open the .java file in Eclipse
- To start the tic -tac-toe game, hit "run" at the top of the eclipse workspace
- Click in any open square to place an x or an o. The turn will be indicated at the top of   the GUI
- An alert will come up at the top of the GUI when a player wins or it is a tie game.
- If you would like to play again, simply click the restart game button at the bottom of the GUI.
